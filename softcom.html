About

We believe real innovation stems from implementing relevant high-tech solutions to actual problems. Thus, by solving the problems around you, in your college, in your society, and in your neighbourhood, you can learn all the skills necessary to be a successful Software Engineer. What’s the point of learning algorithms if you don’t know where to use them. What’s the point of having ideas if you cannot implement them? So learn to give shape to your ideas by learning the tools necessary. All software-related issues of student activities is taken care of by SoftCom. This includes making the Websites and Apps of the fests of the college. There are many other projects which aim at improving the working of the college. Some of them include - The attendance app, Previous year question papers Upload Download Portal, etc. If you have any idea about any project, contact Software Community.
